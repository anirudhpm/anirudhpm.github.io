Arail - Augmented reality
passenger navigation system

brief

The brief was to design an augmented railway platform navigation system for passengers in regards to the booking. This aim to enable and empower every individual with the necessary information to have an enhanced experience.

To identify a passenger's pain points while searcihng for necessary information in the railway station, a brief brainstorming was done with a group of people and sorted down the responses according to the journey milestones of the passenger.
